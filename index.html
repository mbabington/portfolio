My Design Process

I combine my passion for storytelling with a user-centered approach to my UX process, validating my findings through testing, bringing physical interactions to life through digital experiences.

Research

Start by gaining a foundational understanding of the client's business and brand goals.

Empathize

Empathizing with the user is the secret weapon to creating a seamless user experience.

Define

Clearly define the scope with a simple problem statement "How might we...?"

Ideate

Rapid prototype initial ideas, limiting attachment and opening the door to better solutions.

Prototype

Cost-effective simulation for user testing potential solutions.

Test + Iterate

Test early and test often, letting users propel the design forward.

About


I’m a New York-based UX designer, who has presented design recommendations (information architecture, content strategy) to the Department of Education, impacting tens of thousands of NYC students and families. I creatively bring the physical world to life whether I'm producing a web package, writing a compelling pitch, or ux-ing a mobile app. I love tackling complicated problems through unique and actionable solutions.

Prior to UX, I was a producer working in cable television and advertising. I have experience planning and managing creative workflows in collaborative team environments and trouble shooting in high-pressure situations. I wanted to bring my storytelling skills into the digital space, so I enrolled in General Assembly’s User Experience Design Immersive to study industry best practices of human-centered design.
