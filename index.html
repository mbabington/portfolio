My Design Process

I combine my passion for storytelling with a user-centered approach to my UX process, validating my findings through testing, bringing physical interactions to life through digital experiences.

Research

Start by gaining a foundational understanding of the client's business and brand goals.

Empathize

Empathizing with the user is the secret weapon to creating a seamless user experience.

Define

Clearly define the scope with a simple problem statement "How might we...?"

Ideate

Rapid prototype initial ideas, limiting attachment and opening the door to better solutions.

Prototype

Cost-effective simulation for user testing potential solutions.

Test + Iterate

Test early and test often, letting users propel the design forward.
